Duck Dynasty stars go treasure hunting on new Fox Nation show

July 2024 · 2 minute read

The Robertson family is up to something new in an exciting search for buried treasure– but who knows what they’ll find along the way? 

Fans of the longtime “Duck Dynasty” franchise are getting a new Fox Nation exclusive, original series featuring the wildly entertaining Robertsons as they’ve never been seen before. The reality series, “Duck Family Treasure,” follows expert duck hunters and recently turned treasure hunters Jase and Jep Robertson as they seek to uncover incredible historical riches with the help of their quick-witted Uncle Si and expert treasure hunter Murry Crowe. With a June 19th premiere date, fans can now watch this exclusive series on Fox Nation.

In the pilot episode, Jase and Jep set out on an adventure to uncover hidden cannons filled with gold in Louisiana. As the brothers continue their search, the series will show their homecoming to West Monroe, their wives getting a little too involved in their treasure hunting, and some unexpected detours as they try to find what they’re looking for. Some other coveted treasures the Robertson brothers will be seeking are buried loot from an 1800s bandit, a famous legend of buried jars of riches known as Evan’s Gold, and even a friend’s lost wedding ring.
