Jimmer Fredette’s Biography

Jimmer Fredette, born on February 25, 1989, in Glens Falls, New York State, USA, to Al and Kay Fredette, is a renowned professional basketball player currently playing for the Shanghai Sharks in the Chinese Basketball Association.

As of early 2018, Jimmer Fredette’s net worth is estimated at $6 million, accumulated from his successful career in basketball.

During his early years, Jimmer showcased his interest in sports, with his family describing him as an extremely determined and competitive individual from a young age.

At Glens Falls High School, he excelled in basketball, becoming the school’s all-time leading scorer. Later, he attended Brigham Young University from 2007 to 2011, majoring in American studies and playing for the basketball team.

Following his college career, he was picked by the Milwaukee Bucks in the 2011 National Football League (NFL) Draft and subsequently traded to the Sacramento Kings, marking the beginning of his professional basketball journey.

Having played for several teams, Jimmer started playing for the Shanghai Sharks of the Chinese Basketball Association in 2016 and continues to excel in his basketball career.

Besides his professional endeavors, Jimmer Fredette is a family man, married to Whitney Wonnacott since 2012, with whom he has one child.
