Anthony Martial is a French professional footballer who is best known for his career as a forward for Manchester United and the France national team. He has also played for teams like Lyon, Sevilla, and Monaco in the past. During the course of his career, Anthony has won several tournaments such as EFL Cup, FA Cup, FA Community Shield, UEFA European Under-19 Championship, UEFA Nations League, UEFA European Championship, and has been the recipient of honors like “Golden Boy”, Manchester United‘s “Players’ Player of the Year”, and Premier League‘s “Player of the Month”.

Anthony Martial Facts

  • Martial’s youth trainers stated that as a kid, he was very quiet and shy.
  • He began his football career at a youth club called CO Les Ulis based in Paris.
  • Another French professional Patrice Evra mentored Martial during his stay at CO Les Ulis.
  • When he was 14, he joined Lyon’s academy.
  • On June 30, 2013, Martial signed a three-year deal with AS Monaco for the price of 5 million euros plus bonuses.
  • On September 1, 2015, Anthony shifted to the English club Manchester United after he penned a four-year deal worth 36 million British pounds with the potential to rise up to 58 million British pounds.
  • He was selected as the PFA Fan’s Player of the Month for September and a Premier League Player of the Month for September during the 2015-2016 season.
  • Martial competed for the U-19 national team of France at the 2013 European Championship in which they finished second behind Serbia.
