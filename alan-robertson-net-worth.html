What is Alan Robertson’s net worth?

Alan Robertson, son of Phil Alexander Robertson and Marsha Kay Robertson, is an American pastor with a net worth of $3 million. The Robertson Family owns Duck Commander Company, which was originally created to sell Phil Robertson’s duck call but has since expanded to a multi-million dollar enterprise selling hunting gear and instructional videos.

The family gained further fame through their reality series “Duck Dynasty” on A&E Network. Alan Robertson serves as a senior pastor in West Monroe, Louisiana and occasionally works for the family business. He was not featured on “Duck Dynasty” until season four, but became a main character from seasons seven through 10.

Alan Robertson: Early Life and Family

Alan Robertson was born in Louisiana in January 1967 to Phil Alexander Robertson and Marsha Kay Robertson. He is the oldest of four siblings, including Jason Silas Robertson, Willie Jess Robertson, and Jules Jeptha Robertson. The Robertson family is best known for owning and operating the Duck Commander Company, which was created to sell Phil Robertson’s Duck Commander duck call.

Alan Robertson’s Career and Business Ventures

Alan Robertson began working for his family’s multi-million dollar hunting gear business, Duck Commander, as a teenager. He later left to focus on ministry work and is now a senior pastor in West Monroe, Louisiana. He has also authored several books with his wife Lisa, including the Duck Commander Devotional and Desperate Forgiveness. Additionally, Alan and Lisa opened a bakery called Miss Kay’s Sweets and Eats in 2016.

The Robertson Family is also known for their reality series, “Duck Dynasty,” which Alan did not join until season four. He became a main character on the show from seasons seven through 10.
