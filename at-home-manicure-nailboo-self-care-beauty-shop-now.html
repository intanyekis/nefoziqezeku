The Perfect At-Home Experience

Getting a flawless manicure at home is easier than you think with Nailboo.

Are you new to the dip manicure method? It’s a pretty straightforward process, but we’ll outline it below so you can get a sense of how it works

  • Coat and Build: Apply the base liquid. Dip the nail into the build powder — that’s what gives the colored powder a solid foundation. Remove excess build powder by tapping and brushing. 
  • Base Coat and Color: Apply base liquid again, one nail at a time. Dip the nail into the colored powder — this is where the magic happens. Remove excess colored powder, same as you did with the build powder.
  • Activate: Apply a thin layer (the thinner, the better!) of activator liquid to your nails. Wait two minutes. Buff the tops and edges of your nails with the four-way nail file that comes in your dip kit. Apply a second thin layer of activator liquid. 
  • Shine: Wait two minutes, then add topcoat for some shine! 

    • Voilá! Ten perfect nails, done in minutes and almost impossible to mess up.
