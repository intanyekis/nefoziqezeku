Biography

Catalina Vallejos was born in Chile on October 10, 1989. Model and television personality who first gained national attention for winning the title of Miss ATP in 2009 and for winning the competition series Calle 7 twice in a row in 2010 and 2011. She has since become a host for the Chilean television channel Etc…TV. Multitalented, she acted alongside Maria Elena Swett in the 2011 telenovela Aquí mando yo. She began her entertainment career competing in beauty pageants such as the Miss Moviestar Open and Corrida Brooks.
