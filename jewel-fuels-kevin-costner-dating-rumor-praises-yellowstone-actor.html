For a few months now, Kevin Costner has been rumored to be dating singer Jewel, after some pictures of the two of them getting cozy in the Caribbean got out. For a long time, save for a few reports, the alleged couple has kept silent about the whole affair. Until now.

Jewel recently gave an in-depth interview where she talked very openly about her career and her personal life, but while she didn't refuse to answer questions about Kevin Costner, she was a lot more secretive about it than about any other subject. Her response was short and ambiguous, and only fueled rumors about the romance.

Jewel's Ambiguous Comments About Kevin Costner

For a few months now there have been rumors about Kevin Costner and Jewel. The singer and the legendary actor were spotted being a bit too flirty for just friends, and while they've mostly kept quiet about the speculation, they haven't denied it either.

When they were first seen together in the Caribbean, Kevin Costner's arms wrapped tightly around the singer, no one believed they were only there for a tennis fundraiser for Jewel's Inspiring Children Foundation.

"There was definitely something going on. They were flirty, and when they were together, it was like they both just lit up," a source said at the time. "You could tell they were trying to be discreet, but anybody who saw them could tell something was going on."

Recently, during an interview, Jewel spoke candidly about many aspects of her life and career, but for all her honesty, she couldn't give a straightforward answer about her relationship with Kevin Costner. Of course, the lack of denial only fueled the rumors, considering that if the rumors were unfounded, she would have simply denied them.

Instead, she praised the actor, saying that "He’s a great person," and she blushed as she addressed the rumors, adding that "the public fascination is intense for sure."

Their Relationship Is Reportedly Not Serious

While neither of them have given a direct answer about their rumored romance, several sources have spoken about it, most confirming that a new celebrity power couple has been born. Still, the relationship is reportedly very new, and neither of them really want anything serious at the moment.

Apparently, it was Jewel who was first attracted to the actor, with a source saying that "Jewel likes herself a man’s man and she is all about Kevin’s country western vibe." The source added that "They are not serious right now, but they are having fun."

Kevin and Jewel travelled to the Caribbean together last month and appeared not to be hiding their new relationship status.

Their romance was a surprise for fans, but apparently it has been orchestrated by a mutual friend who was convinced they would make a great couple. Millionaire Richard Branson, a mutual friend of the stars, allegedly decided to play matchmaker, and he apparently did a great job.

"Richard invited Jewel to hold her charity fundraiser on Necker and, honestly, the hookup happened organically. They hit it off well and Jewel seems to be happy about it." The new relationship was good for the singer, with the insider claiming that "She is not really one for words, but her friends can see a light in her that hasn’t been there in quite some time."
