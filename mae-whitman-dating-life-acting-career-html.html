Mae Whitman's Dating Life Separating Fact from Fiction

August 2024 Â· 3 minute read

Mae Witman is an American actress and singer who gained prominence as a child actor after her debut in When a Man Loves a Woman (1994) at the age of four. She came to the limelight for her recurring role as Ann Veal on Fox's Arrested Development and Amber Holt on NBC's Parenthood. 

When the 32-year-old actress was asked whether she has a 'type' in her choice for a date in an interview with Glamour, the beautiful young actor responded, saying that it's constantly changing. She likes everyone around, and there is remarkable potential in everyone she meets! 

Want to know more about her dating life? We've got you covered!

Who Has Mae Whitman dated?

According to sources, Whitman was in a relationship with a 34-year old Canadian actor, Dov Tiefrnbach, since 2008. They maintained a low-key dating life. But the couple broke up later, without citing any explanation to the fans.

As per the reports of Heavy, Whitman was also dating Landon Pigg, a noted singer and songwriter. She even helped him compose songs. The alleged couple had also appeared in some episodes of Parenthood. They shared a relationship during the shooting of this family drama. 

In 2014, Mae revealed that they maintained a friendly equation post their breakup and referred to Landon as her favorite guest star. In her interview with Glamour, she also refrained from commenting anything about her present love life. 

Nevertheless, it was also rumored that The DUFF-star was dating Haley Joel Osment in 2002. 

Busting Rumors About Her Private Life

Many rumors are brewing up about the Good Girls star Whitman. She is very discreet about her private life, and she has never opened up much about whom she dates in social media or public forums. 

However, the Wonder Girl has confessed that she has an attraction for both men and women though she has never dated anyone of the same sex. To put it in another way, at least she has never been public about dating any girl.

Since her relationship with Pigg failed, she became more secretive and discreet about her dating life and relationships. In 2014, many people speculated that she was dating Peter Krause.

They were spotted together frequently, and as per the reports, the two shared beautiful chemistry. But they never made an appearance as a couple. Whitman refers to him as her BFF and uses an adorable pet name, 'little one,' to refer to him.

Whitman's Flourishing Career

Born on 9th June 1988, the actor has bagged more than 150 acting credits with roles in television, film work, and also as a voice-over artist. Her first notable voice work was in Avatar as Katara. 

As for the big screen, she has delivered spellbinding performances in The DUFF, Scott Pilgrim vs. the World. From a supporting turn in Arrested Development to Good Girls' lead role, Whitman has come a long way to be in the spotlight. No wonder she has successfully kept her love life under wraps.
