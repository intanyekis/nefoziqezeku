When and where India Everett was born; her childhood, parents, age, and wiki

India Adlis Everett was born on July 15, 1992, in St. Paul, Minnesota, USA. She will be 30 in 2023, and her zodiac sign is Cancer. Everett is the daughter of fight attendant Linda Stewart. Her father, on the other hand, is a mystery. Similarly, the celebrity has a sibling, the identity of whom is similarly unknown.

Furthermore, when India was a child, she moved to Vashon Island in Seattle, Washington. She had a good upbringing and was well-versed in athletics, academics, and creative sectors such as arts from a young age.

In terms of ethnicity, the actress is of Caucasian descent and of American nationality.

Her stature

India Everett is approximately 5 feet 9 inches tall and weighs approximately 59 kg/129lbs.

Academic Qualifications in India

The actress is a well-educated woman. She attended one of the most prominent journalism institutions, the University of Missouri, after graduating from high school. She earned a degree in TV and Radio Broadcast Journalism.

India Everett’s Professional Career: From Journalist to Actress/Model

From 2011 to 2013, India worked as a reporter and anchor for Komu-Tv shortly after graduation. Later, for around five months, she worked as a sports commentator and reporter, and she was named Missouri Professional Communicator of the Year in 2012. She eventually worked as a main anchor and producer for Fox in the Film, Television, and Sports department.

Similarly, after leaving Fox, India began modeling and had a consistent photoshoot with Zulily. She received a contract with BMG Models LA in 2017 for advertising, modeling, and stage performance. Similarly, she portrayed the lead part of Kat in the indie short film Behind Closed Hearts. Everett also made a brief appearance in the season finale of Modern Family. She was also cast in a nonspeaking part in Once Upon A Time In Hollywood opposite Brad Pitt and Margot Robbie.

Her Actress/Model Career

Her previous film appearances include a sex trafficking victim in I Am Not For Sale: The Fight To End Human Trafficking and a nonspeaking role in Top Gun: Maverick. She has also appeared in a number of television shows, including Killing Time (2019), Bulge Bracket (2020), and Dawn of the Zombie Apocalypse (2020).

The multi-talented actress was featured in Swimsuit Illustrated Magazine in June 2018, and she was named Female Model of the Year at the UP(st) ART Creative Awards in December of the same year. India was also included in Maxim Magazine’s ‘What A Beautiful Woman Wants’ issue in July 2019. In addition, she appeared on the cover of Millennium Magazine in August/September 2019.

She is a People Content Manager for Media.Monks as of 2023. She works in multimedia content development, generating videos and social media posts, as well as authoring material and briefs.
