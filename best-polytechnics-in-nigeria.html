Top 10 Best Polytechnics In Nigeria (2023)

May 2024 · 6 minute read

What are the best polytechnics in Nigeria? In Nigeria, the educational system is improving from just learning to practicals. This is so because involving a practical aspect in every learning is important. As such, people are deviating from just having the knowledge to putting the knowledge into practice.

However, a lot of Universities in Nigeria just pass knowledge and expect the student to figure out themselves with the knowledge. But polytechnics in Nigeria join knowledge with technology.

The technology aspect brings students closer to what they are been taught. Most times people consider the study at a polytechnic to be the same as skill acquisition.

All the polytechnics in Nigeria give their best and as such none of them will not be mentioned as the best but every outstanding one will stand.

Just the same way we have the best universities in Nigeria, that is also how we have the best polytechnics in Nigeria. Though there are more than 50 good polytechnics in Nigeria, this content is focusing on just the top 10.

1. Yaba College of Technology (YABTECH)

Because of its unique characteristics and historical context, Yabatechis the best polytechnic in Nigeria. The technological institution has been able to significantly improve the practical training of polytechnic students.

Since its founding in 1947, when it was named “Yaba higher College” and was located in Yaba, Lagos State, this has been the case. In 1973, the name was changed to Federal Polytechnic Yaba.

With the current state of the nation, it has recently increased the number of practical courses that are available to students and pertinent to them. (Nigeria).

currently, Yabatech is the best polytechnic in Nigeria since its scientific publications and communications are of a higher caliber than those of other polytechnics.

2. Federal Polytechnic Ilaro

The second on the list of the top 10 best polytechnics in Nigeria is Federal Polytechnic Ilaro located in Ogun State.

However, it is one of the best polytechnics in Nigeria because it has several learning structures including the following

  • an information and communication technology center
  • an administrative building and annexes
  • a school’s record and data control unit
  • schools for engineering and environmental studies.

Also, it offers free Wi-Fi internet access that is accessible to both teachers and students.

Additionally, they provide a supportive setting for instruction and practice. There are equal admissions opportunities for male and female students each year at the Federal Polytechnic in Ilaro. The number of pupils admitted there has increased recently.

3. Lagos State Polytechnic (LASPOTECH)

One of the best and outstanding polytechnics in Nigeria is Lagos State Polytechnic also referred to as LASPOTECH. However, it is a government-owned tertiary institution located in Ikorodu, Lagos State, Nigeria.

Recently, there have been improvements made to the academic and practical programs offered by the polytechnic, which will help the students grow and become more noticeable in society. Additionally, it accepts students of either gender.

4. Auchi polytechnic, Edo State

On this list is Auchi located in Edo State. The Polytechnic has numerous practical courses available which made it to be one of the best polytechnics in Nigeria.

Also, it has standard and appropriate facilities for those courses and professors with relevant practical experience who can provide the students with the necessary instruction.

However, the school also boasts a calm atmosphere, a well-organized school system, and improved educational tools.

The teachers at the polytechnic are enthusiastic and eager to instruct pupils using any available practical techniques or methodologies. Undergraduate and graduate programs in business, technology, and the arts are available.

The steady electricity at Auchi Polytechnic is beneficial to the advancement of technology. It also follows a gender and social inclusiveness policy, accepting both male and female students equally.

5. Federal polytechnic Ado Ekiti

This polytechnic’s recent improvements to its educational standards, architectural designs, historically significant information, etc., have had a significant positive impact on the growth of the country.

For its students, it offers both full-time and part-time Higher National Diploma (HND) and National Diploma (ND) certificates. Every aspect of it, including the number of students, has grown since the school’s founding in 1978.

6. Federal polytechnic Nekede (FEDPONEK)

Among the best polytechnics in Nigeria is Federal Polytechnic Nekede. Recently, it has advanced to become among the best at creating educational strategies, emphasizing practice over theory.

The institution offers practical education there including

  • The schools of business and management technology,
  • Industrial and applied sciences
  • Environmental design technology
  • Humanities and social sciences
  • Engineering technology.

Because the course programs are divided into two lecture sessions (morning and evening lecture periods). This made the school pleasant for all of the students, the practical courses are open to all students (whether full-time or part-time students).

7. Federal polytechnic Ede

Federal Polytechnic Ede is a prestigious institution with many accomplishments, accolades, and credentials that have elevated it beyond other polytechnics in Nigeria.

It provides useful facilities and good experience in actual practice. It recently created a reliable electrical supply to support technological advancement.

8. Federal polytechnic Bauchi

Presently, Federal Polytechnic Bauchi located in Bauchi state is among the best polytechnics in Nigeria.

This polytechnic includes a sizable plot of land with industry-recognized facilities for top-notch instruction and practicals.

Also, it provides a variety of practical courses that allow students the chance to receive top instruction in a variety of professions.

9. Moshood Abiola polytechnic Abeokuta (MAPOLY)

Today, it is a fantastic school with excellent resources for students to acquire a variety of skills and receive high-quality instruction. The advancement of science and technology is a significant component of operations at this polytechnic.

This is because our society has recently placed a high value on modernization. This polytechnic was established by the federal government so that technical education might serve as the foundation for technological advancement.

The Ogun State government, under which this polytechnic is located, invests in it because it thinks that training technical personnel is one of the justifiable investments necessary for societal transformation.

10. Petroleum Training Institute Effurun

The last on the list of the top 10 best polytechnics in Nigeria is Petroleum training institute Effurun.

Due to their proficiency in the oil and gas sector, graduates from this institution are frequently hired in that sector. It instructs students in business studies, electrical engineering, petroleum and natural gas processing, and other topics.
