Overall Meaning

The lyrics of Santana Chris Stapleton's song Joy speak to the experience of finding inner happiness after a period of struggle. The opening lines suggest that something within the singer is intensifying and giving them newfound confidence, even though things were previously not going well. The walls that had been built around love are falling and the singer is experiencing joy.


The chorus repeats the word "joy" over and over again, emphasizing that the singer is overwhelmed by this emotion. The lines "rolling like the thunder rumbles" and "flying on the wings of angels" suggest that this joy is all-encompassing and powerful. The singer also acknowledges past pain by mentioning "teardrops" and "hate," but has now found peace and is able to let those negative feelings crumble away.


In the second verse, the singer dives deeper into the feeling of joy, describing how it permeates every minute and hour of their life. They are able to push through good and bad times because they believe in the joy they have found within themselves.


Overall, the song is an uplifting celebration of the power of joy to overcome obstacles and transform one's perspective.
