About Domino Kirke: Wife of Penn Badgley, Her Career and Relationship

Penn Badgley, known for his compelling portrayal of Joe Goldberg in the popular Netflix series “You,” tied the knot with Domino Kirke in a New York courthouse on February 27, 2017. Domino Kirke is not merely Penn Badgley’s spouse; she is also a singer, doula, and the founder of Carriage House Birth.

Born in 1983, Domino Kirke initially pursued a music career before branching out into professional doula work and entrepreneurship. She is also the proud mother of Badgley’s child, James, born in August 2020.

Who is Domino Kirke?

Domino Kirke, aged 40, is the daughter of Simon Kirke, a former drummer of the rock band Free. Her musical journey has involved collaborations with renowned artists such as Regina Spektor and The Strokes. In addition to her singing career, she ventured into professional doula work and established Carriage House Birth, a doula training institution, showcasing her diverse expertise in both the arts and healthcare.

The romance between Penn Badgley and Domino Kirke commenced in 2014. Their connection was immediate upon being introduced by mutual friends. They exchanged vows in 2017 and continue to nurture their relationship, capturing the fascination of many as one of Hollywood’s beloved couples.

Penn Badgley is recognized for more than just “You.” His diverse filmography includes “The Day After Tomorrow” and “Easy A.” His portrayal of Joe Goldberg has garnered critical acclaim, and he is currently involved in new projects, ensuring a continued upward trajectory in his career.
