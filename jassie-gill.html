Jassie Gill is an Indian singer and actor who has mainly worked in Punjabi and Hindi language music and films. Over the years, he has been cast in movies such as Mr & Mrs 420, Mundeyan Ton Bachke Rahin, Dil Vil Pyaar Vyaar, Oh Yaara Ainvayi Ainvayi Lut Gaya, Panga, Dildariyaan, Channo Kamli Yaar Di, Mr & Mrs 420 Returns, Sargi, Happy Phirr Bhag Jayegi, High End Yaariyan, Kya Meri Sonam Gupta Bewafa Hai?, and Dare and Lovely. Moreover, Jassie Gill’s discography consists of albums and tracks like Batchmate, Laden, Lancer, Batchamte 2, Churiyan, Teri Je Na Hoyi, Vigre Sharabi, College, Mangeya Ae Dil, Bapu Zimidar, Gabbroo, Bournvita, Guitar Sikhda, Surma Kaala, Keh Gayi Sorry, Naina Nu, and Tamanna Meri.
