New Couple Alert! Jamie Foxx &amp; Stacey Dash Are A Super Sexy Duo! Hollywood Life

September 2024 · 2 minute read

It’s HollyLove for Jamie Foxx and Stacey Dash! Do you think they look good together?

Jamie Fox, 42, has a new woman! Actress Stacey Dash, 44 — who you probably know as Alicia Silverstone‘s quirky best friend Dionne in Clueless — was on Jamie’s arm all night after the Oscars!

After The Academy Awards on Mar. 7, Jamie brought Stacey to the 18th Annual Elton John AIDS Foundation Oscar Viewing Party at the Pacific Design Center in Beverly Hills, and then also took her to The Vanity Fair Oscar Party at the  Sunset Tower Hotel in West Hollywood!

Not a bad way to kick off a relationship, especially when Stacey’s dealing with a recent split from her third husband, Emmanuel Xuereb, according to the NY Daily News. After marrying in 2007, things just weren’t working out for the couple.

Now, Stacey’s with Jamie and it seems like a pretty good match. Jamie is good friends with Stacey’s cousin Damon Dash, former CEO and co-founder of Roc-A-Fella Records and even collaborated with him and Kanye West on the hit “Gold Digger” back in 2005.

So, it looks like both Stacey and Jamie have found something more in their relationship. Not only do they look super happy together in the pics from the parties this past weekend, but now they can both help each others’ careers. Stacey can support Jamie’s next album and Jamie can help Stacey restart her acting career … a match made in heaven!
