Tom Parker Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Tom Parker was born on June 26, 1909 in Breda, Noord-Brabant, Netherlands as Andreas Cornelius Van Kuijk. He is known for his work on Jailhouse Rock (1957), Blue Hawaii (1961) and Elvis: That's the Way It Is (1970). He was married to Loanne Miller and Marie Francis Mott. He died on January 21, 1997 in Las Vegas, Nevada, USA. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿

8Became Elvis Presley's manager in 1956, shortly after having managed to sell his Sun Records contract to RCA for a then unheard $35,000. He got a 25% share of Elvis' paycheck. Because he also had shares in a number of activities centered on Elvis' image (memorabilia, souvenirs, song publishing), he actually made more money out of Elvis than Elvis himself did.
