Mick Jagger Is Engaged To Baby Mama Who Is 43 Years His Junior

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Happily ever after? Mick Jagger and his baby mama, Melanie Hamrick are engaged and happier than ever, despite Hamrick being 43 years Jagger's junior.

After being spotted out last week with a large piece of bling on her finger, according to The Daily Mail, speculation abounded that music superstar Jagger had finally proposed to his long-time girlfriend, Hamrick. And while the former professional ballerina initially denied rumors that the two were officially engaged, sources close to Hamrick have confirmed otherwise.

"Melanie was at the American Ballet Theater and she was telling her friends she was engaged," a source explained. "She was very clear her ring is an engagement ring and she is now his fiancée. Her family also understands them to be engaged and are thrilled."

Jagger and Hamrick have been together since 2014 and share a six-year-old son, Deveraux Octavian Basil Jagger.

This marks the third engagement for the never-retiring Jagger, who met Hamrick in Tokyo after his previous fiancée, L'Wren Scott tragically committed suicide. But given the caginess of their answers to the press, it is hard to tell if wedding bells will be heard anytime in the near future.

Mick Jagger And Melanie Hamrick Have Been Cagy To The Press About Engagement

After Hamrick was seen with a large diamond on her left ring finger last week, she was questioned about whether wedding plans were in the works to become Mrs. Jagger. But instead of talking about how thrilled she was to be engaged, Hamrick instead claimed that she was given a "promise ring" instead.

"I mean, I have to giggle because did Mick give me the ring? Yes. Is it for that finger? Yes," Hamrick explained. "But are we like, horny teenagers where we’re giving each other… In my mind, it's a promise ring. We'll be immature and call it a promise ring."

When pressed about whether she and Jagger will ever get married, Hamrick stated, "I don’t know. I'm kind of like, a live-in-the-moment type of person, so who knows."

It could be that because Hamrick and Jagger have no plans to marry in the near future if at all, they are not using the term "engaged" or "fiancé." And even though there have been engagement rumors in the past, with several sources confirming the two are betrothed, this time the rumors may just be true.

Mick Jagger And Melanie Hamrick Have Dealt Engagement Rumors In The Past

In November Jagger and Hamrick were rumored to be engaged after Hamrick took a selfie that showed a beautiful diamond on her finger.

"A Ballerina hairdo goes with everything," Hamrick captioned her Instagram photo.

However, the focus was not on the prima ballerina's hair. It was on her ring finger, where eagle eye followers spotted two diamond rings.

"Is that an engagement ring?" one fan asked.
