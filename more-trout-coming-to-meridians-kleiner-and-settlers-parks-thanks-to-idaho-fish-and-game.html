More trout coming to Meridian's Kleiner and Settlers parks thanks to Idaho Fish and Game

May 2024 · 1 minute read

MERIDIAN, Idaho (CBS2) — The City of Meridian says anglers can expect more trout in both Kleiner Park and Settlers Park ponds in the coming weeks.

Idaho Fish and Game will stock both the Kleiner Park and Settlers Park ponds the week of Nov. 6 and again the week of Nov. 27 with catchable-size trout.

Kleiner Park will receive 450 fish both times, and Settlers Park will receive 125 fish each.

Anyone over 14 who wishes to fish must have a valid Idaho fishing license to fish in Idaho.
