Keke Palmer Wears Oversized Denim Jumpsuit on Red Carpet: Pics

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Eat your hearts out, Britney and Justin! Keke Palmer just took head-to-toe red carpet denim to another level at a screening for Ice Age: Collision Course Saturday, July 16, in L.A.

The Scream Queens actress, 22, hit the carpet in an oversized denim jumpsuit by Off-White, complete with a statement collar and cuffed sleeves. The utilitarian-inspired piece was cinched at the waist and at both ankles — the better to highlight her slim frame. She left the top unbuttoned, flashing a hint of a nude Fleur du Mal bodysuit underneath. Adding a touch of sparkle, Palmer accessorized with metallic Sergio Rossi pumps, a Messika Paris bracelet and rings, and two choker necklaces — one by Eddie Borgo, one by Pamela Love.

So thankful!! So much gratitude for the magic that is life. ☀️.”

Palmer wasn’t the only star on the scene. Jennifer Lopez was also in attendance, clad in a little white Vatanika dress with a choker neckline and lace accents, plus a Tyler Alexandra envelope clutch and Norman Silverman diamond rings.

Palmer and Lopez are both reprising their roles in the latest film in the beloved animated series. The former Nickelodeon star voices Peaches, Manny (Ray Romano) and Ellie’s (Queen Latifah) teenage mammoth. Meanwhile, Lopez plays Shira, a love interest for saber-tooth tiger Diego (Denis Leary).
