Who is Kia Proctor and What is She Known For?

Kia Proctor, also known as Hazel, is the former girlfriend of NFL quarterback Cam Newton. Before gaining attention due to her relationship with Newton, Proctor worked as a model and dancer. Over the years, she has maintained a relatively low profile, preferring to stay out of the spotlight despite her association with the renowned athlete.

Kia was originally born Shakia Proctor on 6th October 1988 in Virginia, Maryland, in the United States. The names of her parents are Jerome Proctor and Anne Maria Proctor.

The pair raised Kia and her siblings in Maryland, where she had her basic education. Shakia is an American citizen and a product of parents who come from different ethnic backgrounds, thus, she has multiple ancestries.

As regards her occupation, Proctor is a model and party host. She used to be a stripper at a well-known strip club called Washington DC Stadium Club in the States. During her dancing days around the pole, she was known as Hazel.

Not much is known about her modeling career but we do know that she has hosted numerous parties in Atlanta, Georgia. Even though Kia is easily recognized for being the girlfriend of Panthers’ Newton, she is highly respected in the showbiz industry for her professionalism both in modeling and event hosting.
